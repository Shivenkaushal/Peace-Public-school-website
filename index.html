About

Peace Public School is an International School in punjab, India, it was opened on 17 July 2008, and is directed by Naresh Kumar Jain, who has directed it since the school was started. Our school is affilation since 2006 and its extension is upto 31/3/2023 .Its area is upto 4.076 acres, or 16496 square meters. Area of playground in sq. meters 14025. Facilities

MARTIAL ARTS ROOM To provide an amiable aura for learning self defense the school has a well equipped martial arts room where children are trained under the guidance of a professional coach.

LABORATORIES (PHYSICS , CHEMISTRY, BIOLOGY) To ensure an ambience for innovation and creativity, the theory classes are supplemented with practical knowledge. To fulfill this purpose regular lab sessions are held under the guidance of an efficient Science faculty.

ASL LAB To help the children attain a desired level of proficiency in communication skills and ASL Lab has been set up in the school. It provides a realistic silent environment under the guidance of a well-qualified faculty and aims to enrich the language skills of the children.

ART ROOM The Art Room fosters the creative and intellectual development of children through classes facilitated by a qualified and experienced Arts faculty. The kids are taught to master different kinds of colours, paints, oil pastels, etc so as to communicate their thoughts through their paintings. The Art Room truly serves as a space to explore the creativity of the kids.

CONFERENCE ROOM The school has a well designed spacious conference room equipped with all the modern amenities like projector,viewport, internet access and a comfortable seating arrangement which is used as a venue for a myriad of activities ranging from seminars,workshops, presentations and meetings.

DANCE ROOM A well equipped, spacious dance room is an additional facility for the children wherein funfilled dance sessions happen regularly yielding moments of cultural joy.

LIBRARY A well stocked library with a plethora of books serves as a learning center and promotes individual learning.It is an epicenter to explore vast realms of knowledge.

MEDICAL ROOM We have a well equipped medical room with a full time trained medical assistant to provide necessary first aid to the students in case of a medical emergency. An annual health check up including dental and eye check up by specialists is a regular feature along with maintaining a medical record of each and every child.

CORRIDOORS To facilitate the process of learning, our school has aesthetically appealing,open and wide corridors which are decked up with beautiful display boards and act as a medium for the children to exhibit their creativity and artistic skills.

MUSIC ROOM The Instrumental music room aims at honing the musical abilities of the children by providing them with musical instruments like flute, violin, keyboard, sitar, guitar. They also get an opportunity to create musical compositions and present them in the various functions of the school.

PREFECTORIAL BOARD To seek the assistance of our students in maintaining discipline and inculcate leadership qualities in them, the school has a prefectorial board for the junior as well as the senior wing. It comprises of the Head Boy,Head Girl, Sports Captain and Vice Captain, House Captains and Vice Captains and the Prefects.

AMPHITHEATER The Amphitheatre at Peace Public School is a marvel worth witnessing. With a seating capacity of over 500 people, it is graded for maximum viewing. The amphitheatre is a platform for students to showcase their hidden talents. The open space theater is thoughtfully planned to inspire a learning experience for academic and cultural activities. The multipurpose amphitheater can be converted into a pool for splash pool parties . It also has a fountain in the middle.

AUDITORIUM The school has a spacious,fully air conditioned auditorium with a comfortable seating arrangement. It can be easily accommodated by one thousand people at a time. It is a beautiful structure with a main hall,a control room and an elevated stage which is beautifully decorated by the art faculty of the school.The lighting system includes dramatic lights,spot lights and a digital sound system.

PLAYGROUNDS Open space, fresh air, voices cheering, and spirits soaring is what you will witness in the playgrounds. These days with little or no play area and time as well, we have ensured that our children do not miss out on it at school. We have a huge play area which includes a basketball ball ground and a skating rink.

ICT LABS Fully networked and equipped with the latest computers. Computers are used not only to teach computer science courses but also as a resource in academic area.

CLASSROOMS Air-conditioned classrooms powered by Smart Class- The world's latest digital educational technology that makes the class a lively learning platform. To nurture intellectual curiosity and stimulate personal growth among children, we believe in smaller class size. We have a maximum of 30 students in each class so that every child gets individual attention and his individual learning needs can be looked after. Mallika Saggoo.
